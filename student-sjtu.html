International Students

Master & Ph.D. Program

To help the international students who have planned to join SJTU for his/her master/Ph.D. degree, I hope that these information will be beneficious to you.

Background

Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) is an international institution located in Shanghai, one of the world’s most international cities. Each year we admit students from nearly 80 countries, making the university a rich and diverse learning environment.

Application details

SchoolDepartments 030 School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering
Major Code 080900
Major Name(1) 电路与系统 Circuits and Systems
Major Name(2) 集成电路设计 Integrated Circuits Design
Duration (full time) 2.5 years (Master) 4-5 years (Ph.D.)
Tutition fee 28,900 RMBYear (Master) 45,500 RMB/Year (Ph.D.)

Important timeline

  • 1st December: Start of Application

  • 31st March : Scholar application http://apply.sjtu.edu.cn

  • 31st May : Self-sponsored applicant

  • September : First Semester

Admission Criteria

  • Non-chinese citizen

  • The physical health status meets the medical examination requirements

  • Completed B.Eng degree (Master) & Master degree (Ph.D.)

  • Degree Transcript

  • Pass the International Examination GRE 320 (total), IELTS 6.0 (overall), TOEFL 85 (overall), Cambridge Certificate in Advanced English 176 (overall), Cambridge Certificate of Proficiency in English 180 (overall), College English Test Band6 510 (overall), NTS GAT General 70%

  • Photocopy of Passport

  • Passport Photo

  • Personal Statement & Research Plan

  • 2 Recommendation Letters (The referee must hold a faculty position with an associate professor title or higher)

Application fees

  • 800 RMB ( 120USD)
